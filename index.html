Analysis of British Airways Customer Reviews

OVERVIEW: I used the British Airways customer review data collected between 2016 to 2023. METHODS: I have focused my analysis on 6 service categories provided by the airline. I have initially cleaned and explored the data in Python (link below). I have generated a very user friendly and interactive dashboard in Tableau (link below) presenting the average customer ratings based on the time series, Continent, type of aircraft, number of reviews and a map displaying the countries from where the data has been collected. Analysis of Airbnb data in Seatle in Tableau

PROJECT DESCRIPTION: I used an existing Airbnb data in Seattle in order to start an airbnb in the area while maximizing profits. OBJECTIVE: Where, when and the size of the house required to start an Airbnb in Seattle and obtain maximum profits. METHODS: 1) I identified the neighborhoods with highest price per night, using zipcode data. I plotted the top 5 neighbourhoods with highest earnings on the Seattle map. 2) I identified the time of the year that gives the maximum yields and 3) I identified average yields based on the number of bed rooms in a house and number of listings according to the number of bedrooms. INFERENCES: In order to earn maximum profits from an Airbnb, I would suggest to open an Airbnb around the downtown area of Seattle, during early spring. We can opt for eithrt 2-3 bedroom houses as they can provide higher yields with a relatively lower competition than a one bedroom house. A 2-3 bedroom house is also associated with lower investment levels when compared to a house with 4-6 bedrooms. Future recommendations: We can further look into the year around occupany and total earnings of Airbnbs based on the number of bedrooms for a deeper understanding of the profits earned and can improvise recommendations accordingly. *Please view the dashboard in full screen mode in order to have a clear visualization

Analysis of air pollution data in Tableau

PROJECT DESCRIPTION: I used the air pollution measurements collected in Montreal area during summer and during winter. Using the box plot, I showed that the levels of air pollution and variability in air pollution are higher during winter than during summer. Among weekdays, the air pollution levels are higher on Fridays, followed by Monday in both seasons. Airport, Highways and petroleum refineries have higher levels of air pollution and this trend is more evident during winter, possibly associated with higher air pollution levels and variability in winter. In addition, Downtown area has highest pollution. *Please view the dashboard in full screen mode in order to have a clear visualization
